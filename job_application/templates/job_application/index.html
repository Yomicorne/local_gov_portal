Local Government Job Recruitment

Job Title: Administrative Assistant

Requirements:

  • Minimum educational qualification: Bachelor's Degree
  • Experience in administrative roles
  • Strong organizational and communication skills

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration or related field
  • Excellent computer skills (MS Office, Excel, etc.)

Responsibilities:

The Administrative Assistant will be responsible for managing office operations, organizing meetings, handling correspondence, and providing support to senior staff members.

How to Apply:

Interested candidates should submit their resume, cover letter, and relevant documents to hr@localgovernment.gov. Please mention "Admin Assistant Application" in the email subject.
